Hi, I'm Britt!


In 2018, I backpacked solo for about 5 months, during this trip I visited 14 countries. Just me and my backpack.


Planning my travels took up a lot of my time. I noticed there wasn't a resource available to travelers, especially backpackers to see how others made their way through a region. Most of my planning ended up happening via word of mouth(do you get the name now?) when talking to others in the hostels.


I wanted to create a platform that would allow users to share and search this valuable information. So here it is. I hope it makes you want to book a ticket, for 2021 of course.
